Lifesteal Mechanics

Master the rules of heart stealing and revival

Heart Stealing

Eliminate players in PvP to steal 1 heart container. Lose all your hearts, and you will be temporarily banned or put in spectator mode!

Crafting Hearts

Combine Netherite Ingots, Totems of Undying, and Diamond Blocks in a crafting table to forge extra Heart Containers (Cap: 30 Hearts).

Revive Beacons

Craft a Revive Beacon to bring back eliminated teammates who have reached zero hearts and lost access to the SMP.

Grace Period

New players receive a 60-minute PvP protection grace period to build a starter base and equip basic gear before entering combat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about playing on Storm Steal

What is the server IP and Port?
Java IP: play.stormsteal.net | Bedrock IP: play.stormsteal.net Port: 19132.
What happens when I run out of hearts?
When you lose all hearts, you are temporarily temp-banned for 24 hours unless a teammate uses a Revive Beacon to bring you back earlier.
Are mods or client hacks allowed?
No. Unfair modifications like X-Ray, Killaura, Fly, and Baritone will lead to a permanent ban. Minimaps and FPS boosters are allowed.
Who is the main Admin of the server?
GlitchBloom is the primary Server Administrator and Owner. Contact them or staff via Discord for support.
