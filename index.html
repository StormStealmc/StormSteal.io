Lifesteal Mechanics

Master the rules of heart stealing and revival

Heart Stealing

Eliminate players in PvP to steal 1 heart container. Lose all your hearts, and you will be temporarily banned or put in spectator mode!

Crafting Hearts

Combine Netherite Ingots, Totems of Undying, and Diamond Blocks in a crafting table to forge extra Heart Containers.

Revive Beacons

Craft a Revive Beacon to bring back eliminated teammates who have reached zero hearts and lost access to the SMP.

Grace Period

New players receive a 60-minute PvP protection grace period to build a starter base and equip basic gear before entering combat.
